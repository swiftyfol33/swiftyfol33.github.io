Joshua Kaplan is a Brooklyn-based freelance designer and advisor, specializing in package design, brand development, product design, user interface, web design, creative direction, and iconography. He studied industrial design at the Ontario College of Art & Design in Toronto, Canada, and holds certification as a super recognizer from Goldsmiths University of London.
